How It Works

Four simple steps to experiencing life through someone else's eyes.

1

Register Your Profile

Create your Retina account and complete your consciousness profile. Our advanced algorithms analyze your neural patterns to ensure optimal swap compatibility.

2

Select Your Experience

Free users are matched with random active avatars, while Premium subscribers can browse and select from available hosts based on preferences like occupation, location, or compatibility ratings.

3

Initiate The Swap

Once matched, enter a comfortable position and initiate the swap sequence. The transition takes approximately 30 seconds, after which you'll wake up in your host body.
